About

I am Assistant Professor at Ecole Polytechnique, where I work in the Epizeuxis team, the Networks research team of LIX laboratory.

From Oct. 2018 to Jan. 2022, I was PhD student in computer science at Toulouse INP in RMESS team of IRIT laboratory under the supervision of Gentian Jakllari and André-Luc Beylot. After that, I held a Postdoctoral Researcher position at INRIA lab, Lille center, where I worked with Nathalie Mitton in the Self-organizing Future Ubiquitous Networks team, from March to Aug. 2022.

Before starting my Ph.D, I was Research Engineer in the same team (Nov. 2017 to Sept. 2018) and Graduate Research Intern in the SEPIA team of IRIT (from March to Aug. 2017). The latter internship was part of my Computer Engineering curriculum at Polytechnique Yaoundé (Cameroon).

My main research interests are wireless networks and ubiquitous computing with applications to localization technologies, mobile healthcare and smart environments.
